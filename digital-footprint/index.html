Jan 27, 2020

THE FOURTH PARADIGM - FROM DATA TO DISCOVERY

Data gathered from various sources ranging from Economics to Biomedical Sciences is having a huge impact on the policy decisions being made in current times and this is only going to increase in the future. An analysis of such large amounts of data gathered from diverse fields requires experts from Mathematics, Engineering, Science, Economics and Social Sciences to work together for the betterment of society. Hence, there is an immediate need to accelerate data based research in these areas. This is The Fourth Paradigm of Science, with the other three being empirical measurements, theoretical modelling and computer simulations.
